    Requirements

    What you'll need

    Driving License

    Valid Malaysian (D) license, or international driving permit for foreign visitors.

    IC / Passport

    Original IC for Malaysian residents, or original passport for foreign nationals. Both will be verified.

    Minimum Age

    21 years and above. Sport / luxury cars require minimum age 25 with at least 3 years driving experience.

    Deposit

    RM 300 for standard cars, RM 500–1,500 for premium. 100% refundable when the car is returned in good condition.
